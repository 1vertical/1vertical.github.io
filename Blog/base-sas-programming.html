The basics of Base SAS Programming

Here we will go through the basics of Base SAS programming. Now you may wonder, what is BASE SAS programming? This refers to the ground or basic level procedures and management of data. In other words, a newbie in management of data. :) Not to worry! You will be guided and tested to apply your knowledge in these exercises. Each lesson builds up on previous knowledge, so if you use this guide as a quick reference guide, it might aid you but the target audience is intended for newcomers to Base SAS. Make sure to PRACTICE this, reading is not enough to make it sink in, nor gaining confidence in any programming language. Sit your butt down, do the work, get your beverage, get comfortable, focus and practice!
