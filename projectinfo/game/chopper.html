Inspiration
The purpose of the prototype is create a similar movement behavior as the games found in the Strike series made for the Sega consoles in the 90s. I've achieved the basic movement without the lerping/dragging when the chopper stops moving. I've also managed to make the camera follow the player.

Moveset Future Improvements
This prototype will serve as the basis if I ever take the project further. Things I would add to complete the moveset are:
  • Juice on movement - animation fundamentals like swaying, squash and stretch, etc.
  • Dynamic elevation based on the distance from the ground
